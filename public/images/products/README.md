### Image Specifications

**Recommended:**
- Size: 1200x1200px minimum (square format works best)
- Format: JPG or WebP
- Quality: High resolution (300dpi preferred)
- Background: Clean, neutral, or lifestyle setting

### Photography Tips

1. **Lighting**: Natural light works best - shoot near a window
2. **Angles**: Show multiple angles for key products
3. **Context**: Including lifestyle shots (items in use) increases appeal
4. **Details**: Close-ups of texture and craftsmanship
5. **Colors**: Ensure colors are accurate and vibrant
6. **Consistency**: Use similar backgrounds/styles across products

### Multiple Images per Product

You can add multiple images for each product by listing them in the product's `images` array in `/src/data/products.json`:

```json
"images": [
  "/images/products/bunny.jpg",
  "/images/products/bunny-2.jpg",
  "/images/products/bunny-detail.jpg"
]
```
